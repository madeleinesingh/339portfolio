Get Active

Exercise improves mental health by reducing anxiety, depression, and negative mood and by improving self-esteem and cognitive function. Exercise has also been found to alleviate symptoms such as low self-esteem and social withdrawal. For these reasons, and many more, exercise is an essential part of a lifestyle focused on wellness. On this page, I’ve included all of my favorite local activities that are sure to get your heart pumping!

Local Studios (Ann Arbor)

After living in Ann Arbor for a couple of years, I have looked near and far for fun, new workouts in local studios. Although classes typically cost money, most of these studios have amazing new member discounts, so they’re definitely worth a try if you’re looking to go out and try something new. Here are some of my all-time favorites:

  • SoulCycle: super fun cycling classes!
  • MVMNT Fit: Offers cycling, HIIT, and strength classes.
  • Barre Code: Offers HIIT classes in three different formats: Barre, Bootcamp, and Brawl
  • City Row: Rowing classes that incorporate strength training.
  • Red Yoga: Offers a variety of yoga classes
  • SolidCore: Unique pilates workout designed to burn fat and build muscle.

Free Fun

For those of us who enjoy being outside, or are balling on a budget, I’ve also taken note of my favorite free activities in Ann Arbor. These are great to do alone or with friends, and most are available any time of the year … weather permitting!

  • Nichols Arboretum ("The Arb"): beautiful property filled with trees and trails.
  • UM Rec Sports: facilities that are free to students and staff.
  • Palmer Field: a great place to run around the track, or just enjoy a nice day outside.
