30-Day Wellness Challenge

If you want to feel better but don’t know where to start, this 30-day wellness challenge has lots of great small changes that you can start implementing into your daily routine. There are both physical and mental wellness tips that overall result in a healthier and more balanced lifestyle.

  • Day 1: Buy a journal and write an entry about what you’re grateful for
  • Day 2: Meditate for 30 minutes
  • Day 3: Try a new healthy recipe
  • Day 4: Set a fitness goal for the next few weeks
  • Day 5: Reach out to someone you haven’t talked to in a while
  • Day 6: Go on a long walk
  • Day 7: Don’t use your phone 30 minutes before bed
  • Day 8: Buy yourself your favorite meal
  • Day 9: Make a list of all of your recent accomplishments
  • Day 10: Do something kind for someone anonymously
  • Day 11: Focus on your posture
  • Day 12: Create a Pinterest board to inspire you
  • Day 13: Listen to a new podcast
  • Day 14: Declutter your bedroom or workspace
  • Day 15: Unsubscribe from unnecessary emails
  • Day 16: Do one thing you’ve been putting off
  • Day 17: Buy flowers for yourself or a friend
  • Day 18: Watch the sunrise or sunset
  • Day 19: Go to bed an hour earlier than usual
  • Day 20: Set your financial goals
  • Day 21: Take 5 deep breaths once every hour
  • Day 22: Eat breakfast or lunch outside
  • Day 23: Take a hot bath
  • Day 24: Get rid of 5 things you never use
  • Day 25: Try a workout you haven’t done before
  • Day 26: Buy coffee for a friend or a stranger
  • Day 27: Drink 8 full glasses of water throughout the day
  • Day 28: Treat yourself to a spa day
  • Day 29: Take a day off from social media
  • Day 30: Reflect on your progress from the last 30 days!
